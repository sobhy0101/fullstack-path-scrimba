How I stay committed to learning

I like to think of myself as a lifelong learner. I used to spend hours and hours learning, then try to create simple projects using what I learned. That would have been great if those projects actually helped me learn what I was trying to learn. But in reality, I wasn't spending enough time figuring out how to integrate different concepts together.

So I started documenting my learning journey here in this blog. By teaching others what I'm learning, I solidify my own understanding and create a resource I can refer back to when I need a refresher.

How I got started

I started simple and gradually grew my learning plan. I would decide on a topic, maybe something I struggled with during a course, and I'd create a blog post about it. The act of writing about it forced me to truly understand the concept. Plus, it's pretty cool to see how far I've come by looking back at my earlier posts!
